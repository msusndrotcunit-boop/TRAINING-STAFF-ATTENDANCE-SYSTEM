About Mindanao State University - Sultan Naga Dimaporo ROTC

The Reserve Officers' Training Corps (ROTC) at Mindanao State University - Sultan Naga Dimaporo is dedicated to developing disciplined, competent, and patriotic citizens. Our training program emphasizes leadership, military skills, and community service.

Core Values:
  • Honor: Upholding integrity and ethical conduct
  • Duty: Fulfilling responsibilities with commitment
  • Country: Serving the nation with patriotism
  • Discipline: Maintaining order and self-control
  • Leadership: Developing effective leadership skills

About the System

The ROTC Training Staff Attendance Management System is a comprehensive digital solution designed to streamline attendance tracking and personnel management for the Mindanao State University - Sultan Naga Dimaporo ROTC Unit.

QR Code Technology

Advanced QR code scanning for instant attendance recording. Each staff member has a unique QR code for quick and accurate check-ins.

Real-time Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards and reports providing insights into attendance patterns, staff activity, and operational efficiency.

Staff Management

Complete personnel management system with profile tracking, rank management, and service record maintenance.

Key Features

  • Unified Login System - Single panel for staff and admin with role-based access
  • QR Code Integration - Generate and scan QR codes for attendance
  • Staff Registration - Complete personnel data management
  • Real-time Analytics - Live dashboards and statistics
  • Responsive Design - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Secure Authentication - Username-based secure login
  • Data Export - Generate reports in multiple formats
  • Military Standards - Designed for ROTC operations
